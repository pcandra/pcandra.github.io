About

My name is Paige Candra and I am a storyteller with a B.A. in Interactive Media Studies and minors in creative writing and marketing. I have experience designing logos and branding. I am a passionate reader and writer with a love for novels both fictional and non-fictional. With my combined graphic, writing, marketing, and social media skills I look to capture the perfect story.
